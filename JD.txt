About the work from home job or internship
Selected intern day to day responsibilities include:

1. Review Raw Footage: Organize and assess video clips and assets.
2. Video Editing for YouTube: Edit long-form videos suitable for YouTube, ensuring a coherent and engaging storyline. Add transitions, effects, text overlays, and graphics to enhance the video. Incorporate background music and sound effects where appropriate.
3. Editing Instagram Content: Create short-form videos optimized for Instagram, including Reels and Stories. Add captions, stickers, and other interactive elements to increase engagement.
4. Incorporate branding elements such as logos, colour schemes, and fonts consistently across all videos.
5. Thumbnail and Graphic Creation: Design eye-catching thumbnails for YouTube videos to maximize click-through rates.
6. Collaborate with Content Creators and research current trends in social media and video editing.
7. Maintain an organized library of video assets and project files. Ensure that all files are backed up and easily accessible to the team
